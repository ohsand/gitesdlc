L'hirondelle

L'hirondelle

A 3 or 4 bedroom gite sleeping 6 or 8

This characterful gite, approached via a wide path from the car park, has a feeling of spaciousness as you enter through large French doors into an atrium which rises from a tiled floor with decorative frieze up to exposed original roof timbers and a galleried landing. The open plan lounge has an airy feel, along with a kitchen and dining area seating up to 8 people. The kitchen is fully equipped with fridge/freezer, dishwasher, microwave and gas cooker. Although the gite does not contain a washing machine, it does have access to on-site laundry facilities.

A spacious en-suite double bedroom is set on the ground floor, as is one of the twin bedrooms. The latter has a French door leading on to the courtyard patio. Upstairs, two further bedrooms, one double and one twin-bedded, can be found at either side of a galleried landing which contains comfortable seating overlooking the atrium and open plan living area, as well as having views across the courtyard.

The particularly spacious double bedroom boasts an impressive French king-sized bed with upholstered head and footboard, and matching furniture. The twin bedroom has dark oak furniture, typical of the region, with prettily co-ordinated furnishings. The bathroom, also on the first floor, comprises walk-in shower, marble-topped vanity basin with lighted mirror and WC, fully tiled and with heated towel rail.

The first floor is fully boarded with chestnut flooring, and all rooms have fitted electric panel heaters. Wifi, French TV, DVD player and a selection of DVDs are available for guests. A cot and high chair are available upon request.

Outside there is a garden with barbecue, table and chairs for al fresco meals, and a patio alongside the courtyard.
