Welcome to

Gites Dans Les Champs

We have three pretty, self-catering holiday cottages, situated in the beautiful countryside of Poitou-Charentes in the west of France. Our gites are set on three acres of private parkland with a stunning swimming pool and sun terrace. Ideally located for walking, cycling, kayaking and horse riding, this relaxing environment is the perfect place to spend time with family and friends. We are a mere 5 minute drive (or a short bike ride) to the local supermarket and boulangeries.
Each gite has access to Wi-Fi internet, french TV, a DVD with a selection of DVDs, board games and a bluetooth speaker. We also have a selection of bikes available for guests to use.
